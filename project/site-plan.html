Final Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Discover Utah County

This site name supports the purpose of the website which is to help visitors discover fun activities and areas of interest located in Utah County, Utah.

Site Purpose:

For the Discover Utah County

The purpose of the website is to help visitors discover fun activities and areas of interest in Utah County. The type of content will include activities, events, and recreation available in Utah County. It will also provide information including cost of the activities, current weather, etc.

Scenarios:

  • What outdoor activities can I do in Utah County?

  • This question will drive content for individuals who are interested in doing outdoor activities such as hiking, swimming, picknicking, etc.

  • What free activities I can do in Utah County?

  • This will drive content for individuals who are interested in doing activities that are free or inexpensive such as museums, parks, etc.

Color Schema:

Two colors that will be used for the color schema are:

  • Everglade (1A412A): This is the primary color and will be used for the header, and the occasional background color.
  • Trendy Dark Green (2F6B3D): This will be used for some headers, accents, and buttons.

Typography:

The fonts that will be used for the typography:

  • Roboto is the main font that will be used. It will be used for headings, paragraphs, and most of the text content.
